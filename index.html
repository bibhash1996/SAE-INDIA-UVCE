SAEINDIA UVCE, A club started by a couple of automobile enthusiasts ,built on passion and the burning desire to achieve something big. Still a young club but the ambitions are nowhere small. EMPOWER UVCE, our brand name will one day be a force to reckon with,and we will work towards making it so.

History

  SAE INDIA UVCE

The story begins when 8 people decided to do something for the branch of engineering that nurtured them so far. This needed to be the step taken, for a young club like SAEINDIA UVCE to grow forth. And that was when, the executive committee decided to go for the National Go Karting Championship 2014. After arranging a recruitment test and conducting interviews, a team of 22 students joined hands to design, innovate upon and fabricate a go kart without any professional help. In 6 months, we were to build a go kart from scratch that weighs less than 200kgs and should cost less than Rs.80,000. Many of us haven't handled cash more than our admission fees.

The UVCE Foundation was generous enough to sponsor the registration fee and that's when things got realistic. After a lot of grunts, midnight oil burning dedicated work, the team participated in the pre final round and won the 7th place all over the country. The Organizers were Mighty Impressed with our work and wished us the best for the finals.

After running around, researching, brainstorming and finalizing we finally got hold of the required raw materials to build the vehicle. The first purchase we made was buying a Honda Shine, the power horse of our project. As high as we could get with our debut effort, things got down to business when we had to invest a lot more to finish building the go kart. Thanks to Foundation for Excellence and Vision UVCE all our financial hurdles were cleared and we were all set to complete the fabrication. And we did so, just hours before the competition started!

After roping in Edwin Joseph (SAE member), "the electronics wizard" from 7th sem ECE we set sail towards Coimbatore where heavy rains and unfamiliar territory welcomed 20 members of our team on a September night. The final round of the competition was for 3 days throughout which it rained continuously. But that did not stop us from performing brilliantly with the
Technical inspection
Business Presentation
Dynamic testing
Endurance rounds

We were so thrilled to see our vehicle finally rolling on the tarmac of kari motor speedway. Sir.M.V.Visvesvaraya's footprints were now on one of the most exclusive sporting venues on the country. This was all made possible due to the hard work and enthusiasm of all the team mates and a special mention to Pratheek. N.H the driver of the team who outperformed other teams.

The team was absolutely delighted when we realized that we came 6th, out of 98 teams. Given that this is the first ever time, SAEINDIA UVCE has stepped out with a plan in mind. With great support from Dr.N.L.Lakshmanaswamy our chairman and Dr.Chittappa, our club counselor we have made it so far successfully. Our team firmly believes that our juniors are capable of winning the next year's competition and we have it in us to succeed everywhere we go. Here's to our hopes that we continue with this success and win ever more!

  SAE INTERNATIONAL

- R&D and facilitates to transportation industries such as automotive, aerospace and commercial vehicles.
- Devotes resources to projects and programs in STEM education (Academic disciplines of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics), Professional certification, and collegiate design competitions.
- The SAE Collegiate Design Series provides an opportunity for college students to go beyond textbook theory and replicates the process of engineering design and manufacturing
- Student teams act as the suppliers and design, build and test a prototype vehicle that they believe meets the customer's specifications.
Each team then presents its prototype to the customer at the annual competitions and is judged on several criteria. The team with the highest points essentially wins the contract.

SAE INDIA

  SAE INDIA

SAE India is an affiliate society of SAE International, registered as an Indian nonprofit engineering and scientific society dedicated to the advancement of mobility community in India. As an individual member driven society of mobility practitioners, SAEINDIA comprises members who are individuals from the mobility community, which includes engineers, executives from industry,government officials, academics and students. Principal emphasis is placed on transport industries such as automotive, aerospace, and commercial vehicles. SAE India holds a variety of competitions every year for engineering students across the country.
• SUPRA SAE India Student Formula
• Baja SAE India
• EFFI Cycle
• Aero Design
• Eco Kart

Club Activities

The club conducts various events during the academic year. Each of our college’s two technical fests will have at least one event organized by the clubs. AUTOQUIZ, Robodrive, Bolt it!! and many more events. Apart from these the club also conducts seminars and demonstrations on the topics of automobiles and automotive tech, software and their uses etc. The main focus of the club is to participate and win in national level competitions conducted by highly recognized organizations like SAE INDIA, ISNEE etc. These events not only help students to think out of the box, but also get them involved in physical work related to their theoretical calculations. These competitions develop the student’s sense of team work, innovation, adaptability and understanding of processes. These competitions are a great way of exposing young creative minds to the industry and its needs.

SAE INDIA BAJA

The BAJA SAE Series® is an event for the undergraduate engineering students, organized globally by the Society of Automotive Engineers, USA. Read more

   SAE INDIA BAJA

http://www.bajasaeindia.org/ The BAJA SAE Series® is an event for the undergraduate engineering students, organized globally by the Society of Automotive Engineers, USA. The event originated in the name of Mini - BAJA, in the year 1976 at University of Carolina. Since then, the event has spanned across six countries – USA, Mexico, South Africa, Korea, Brazil and India. The BAJA SAE tasks the students to design, fabricate and validate a single seater four - wheeled off road vehicle to take part in series of events spread over a course of 3 days that test the vehicle for the sound engineering practices that have gone into it, the agility of the vehicle in terms of gradability, speed, acceleration and maneuverability characteristics and finally its ability to endure that back breaking durability test. After not qualifying in the first attempt, the team has regrouped, redesigned and is ready to attend the 2017 edition of this challenge. No stone is left unturned in the pursuit of making a car better than the competitors.

   Go-Kart Design Challenge

http://www.gkdc.in/ Go-Kart Design Challenge- is a contest formulated to enhance the approach of students practicing Engineering and Diploma courses. This event is fundamentally all about designing and fabricating a Go Kart at very low expenditure. We expect the teams to manufacture Go Karts yielding optimum performance. The teams participating in this contest have to go through two rounds, Pre-Final Round (PFR) and Final Round (FR). It will be mandatory for all the teams to clear the PFR in order to advance to the FR. Teams are required to manufacture their Go Karts without any direct/indirect involvement of any kind of Professional help.
